## Features

- **Add Group Members** - Create a group of people to split with
- **Track Expenses** - Add expenses with description and amount
- **Flexible Splitting** - Choose who paid and who to split with
- **Auto Balances** - See who owes what at a glance
- **Smart Settlements** - Minimized transactions to settle up
- **Local Storage** - Data persists in your browser
- **Export Data** - Download expense history as JSON
- **Mobile Friendly** - Works great on any device

## Use Cases

- Splitting rent and utilities with roommates
- Group vacation expenses
- Restaurant bills with friends
- Event planning costs
- Any shared expenses!

## How It Works

1. **Add members** - Enter names of people in your group
2. **Add expenses** - Enter what was bought, how much, who paid
3. **Select split** - Choose who should share the cost
4. **View balances** - See who owes money and who is owed
5. **Settle up** - Follow the suggested payments

## Example

```
Group: Alice, Bob, Carol

Expenses:
- Dinner $90 (paid by Alice, split 3 ways)
- Groceries $60 (paid by Bob, split 3 ways)
- Movie $45 (paid by Carol, split 3 ways)

Result:
- Alice: +$25 (is owed)
- Bob: -$5 (owes)
- Carol: -$20 (owes)

Settlements:
- Carol pays Alice $20
- Bob pays Alice $5
```

## Tech Stack

- Vanilla JavaScript
- CSS3 with modern design
- LocalStorage for persistence
- No backend required

## Privacy

All data is stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
